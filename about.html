professional —

Back in 2018, while working towards my Bachelor's degree in Cognitive & Computer Sciences at McGill University, I began as an intern on a web-based software called LORIS. LORIS is a research platform specializing in the storage, processing and dissemination of large-scale behavioural and neuroimaging data. After graduating, I became a full-time member of one of their private instance teams that catered to the Canadian Consortium of Neurodegenration and Aging (CCNA) . I quickly worked my way to becoming the technical lead of this project, and directed a team of 3 developers.
